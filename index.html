About

I am a dedicated landscape architect with 2 years of experience in creating innovative and sustainable outdoor spaces. My journey in landscape architecture began with a degree in Landscape Architecture from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture & Technology(JKUAT), where I developed a deep appreciation for the harmony between nature and design. I believe in creating landscapes that not only meet the needs of clients but also contribute positively to the environment and community.
